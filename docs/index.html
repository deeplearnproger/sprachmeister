Privacy Policy

Data Collection

SprachMeister is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with a great learning experience.

  • Your voice recordings are processed locally on your device
  • Writing exercises are analyzed using secure AI services
  • Progress data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

Third-Party Services

To provide AI-powered language analysis, we may use the following services:

  • Speech-to-Text services for voice recognition
  • AI language models for conversation and correction
  • Text-to-Speech services for pronunciation examples

These services process data according to their respective privacy policies. No personal identifying information is shared with these services.

Data Storage

All your learning progress, conversation history, and writing exercises are stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

SprachMeister does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does voice practice work?
    Tap the microphone button to start speaking. The AI will listen and respond naturally, helping you practice real conversations.
  • Can I practice offline?
    Some features require an internet connection for AI processing. Your progress is always saved locally.
  • What German level is this app for?
    SprachMeister is suitable for all levels, from A1 beginners to C2 advanced learners.
  • How do I reset my progress?
    Go to Settings within the app and select "Reset Progress" to start fresh.
